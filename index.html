A community built around balance.

Every gathering is thoughtfully put together — beautiful spaces, calm energy, and zero pressure. If the weather turns, we simply move the magic indoors.

Good to know

Questions, answered

Do I need to be experienced to join pilates?

Not at all. Every session is all-levels and beginner-friendly — our instructors offer modifications so you can move at your own pace.

What happens if it rains?

Every outdoor event has an indoor backup. If the forecast turns, we move to our indoor space at the same time and email you the updated location in advance.

Can I come on my own?

Absolutely — most people do. Our events are designed to be warm and welcoming, and you'll leave having met a few good people.

What's included in the ticket?

It varies by event, but typically your spot, any equipment (like mats), and the food or drinks listed on the event. Details are in your confirmation email.

What's your refund policy?

Spots are limited, so we ask for at least 48 hours' notice to offer a credit toward a future event. Reach out any time and we'll sort it.
